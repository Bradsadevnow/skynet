# Volcanic Bond Complex

**Villain:** How To Survive Campus
**Category:** prestige_lair
**Property Type:** fortified caldera residence
**Location Vibe:** impossible wealth with active magma denial
**Access Pattern:** one theatrical transit route plus several lethal maintenance shafts
**Defense Logic:** the environment is doing half the threatening for free
**Hospitality Cover:** private leadership retreat
**Primary Vulnerability:** every system assumes the monologue will not be interrupted

## Lair Summary
A lair that mistakes dramatic geography for invulnerability and keeps almost getting away with it.

## Functional Features
- Secret Entrance / Bookcase Passage: lets access feel like revelation instead of ordinary circulation
- Over-Elaborate Security: transforms defense into spectacle while creating memorable failure points
- Self-Destruct Mechanism: gives the climax a countdown and makes escape as important as victory

## Story-Function Logic
- Self-Destruct Exists For The Countdown: closes the story through urgency rather than cleanup logistics
- Catwalk Exists For The Final Fight: makes the climax bodily, vertical, and fall-friendly

## Meta Voice
clients expect to see such lairs
