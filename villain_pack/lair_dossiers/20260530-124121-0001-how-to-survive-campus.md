**Villain:** How To Survive Campus
**Category:** landscape_threshold_lair
**Property Type:** ritualized wild site
**Location Vibe:** predestination through terrain and weather
**Access Pattern:** wandering path, stone marker, omen, and one crossing nobody can later map cleanly
