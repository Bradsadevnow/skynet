# Catastrophe Procurement Spiral

**Villain:** How To Survive Campus
**Category:** heist_adjacent
**Phase:** moving inventory under cover of crisis
**Target Surface:** grants, shipments, relics, relief funds
**Betrayal Surface:** logistics lead, procurement clerk, or underpaid sidekick
**Twist Shape:** the rescue corridor was built to become a theft corridor
**Failure Condition:** the paper trail outlives the adrenaline

## Scheme Summary
The villain exploits a live emergency to route assets, artifacts, or personnel into private control.

## Story Mechanics
- plot goal: Take Over the City (scaled-down domination goal that keeps stakes intimate and grounded)
- plot shape: The Plan Was to Be Captured (treats imprisonment or surrender as the real point of entry)
- betrayal mode: The Starscream (makes the lieutenant's ambition an internal threat to the whole organization)
- reversal mode: Peripeteia (flips fortune so abruptly the story must reinterpret the last several minutes at once)
- satirical garnish: Ass Pull

## Core Moves
- call panic preparedness
- move goods through emergency exceptions
- turn oversight fatigue into clearance
