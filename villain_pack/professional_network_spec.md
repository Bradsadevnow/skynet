# Villain Professional Network V1

## Product Shape

This is a world generator disguised as a profile generator.

The core loop is:
1. generate villain
2. derive institution page
3. derive company page
4. derive job posting
5. let each artifact point to the others

## V1 Constraint

Do not build social feeds, endorsements, recommendations, alumni networks, or recruiter tooling yet.
They should be implied rather than first-class.

The Zillow lane is intentionally adjacent rather than separate: property listings are just operating environments with better lighting and a worse disclosure regime.

## Why This Works

Each entity creates demand for the next one.
That creates a graph rather than a pile of one-off jokes.
