Amidst wine‑laden conversations about basements as legal relatives, an empty gazebo barks at night, and a ceramic owl flutters from kitchen island to marina tackle rooms, the county’s hidden histories surface. The finale leaves an open wound: the ceramic owl returns, whispering of vineyard fires, winter storms, and a sequel that will drag every resident’s secrets back into the glare of the courthouse window.
