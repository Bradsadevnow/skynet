## Synopsis

Jenna Hawthorne, the charismatic marina manager with a past as murky as the tide, is drawn into Derek’s orbit when a Christmas ornament turns wet on the kitchen island—an omen whispered in the wind. In this gothic divorce of trust, every scene is a tightrope walk between confession and concealment, culminating in an explosion of truth that could either heal or irrevocably split the community.
