Elias Sutton, an overextended lawyer haunted by an implosion of her past, moves into the Marina Tackle Room—old family things whispered in the walls. She meets Tess Hawthorne, a divorced coach with a dangerous charm, Derek Vale who hides evidence in his toolshed, and Jenna Winterson, a therapist whose silence is as thick as winter bees. In this world, suspicion is infrastructure and guilt is never proven—only feared.
