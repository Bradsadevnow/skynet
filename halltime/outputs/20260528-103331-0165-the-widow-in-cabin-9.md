## Synopsis

Tess Dane retreats to her inherited cabin after a public implosion that shattered not only her bakery but the fragile trust she had with herself. A child’s crude drawing of stairs—an omen of descent—appears in the kitchen, while a casserole dish, once a symbol of nourishment, becomes a relic of betrayal. In the climax, an unopened letter ties the Widow to Blackthorn Vineyard, St. Agnes Regional, and a brunch resurrection event, while a ceramic owl reappears to signify a cyclical haunting. The story ends with the sheriff discovering fresh mud on the boat key, hinting at deeper mysteries that will spill into future HallTime tales.
