## Synopsis
What My Sister Buried plunges the quiet enclave of Blackthorn County into a feverish holiday noir where grief, deceit and arson intertwine like tangled vines on an abandoned orchard. Elias Hawthorne, once a bakery owner whose dough was as stubborn as her secrets, inherits an old storm shelter that holds more than cracked insulation— it houses a buried trauma she never confronted. When Gavin Marlowe, the regional dentist with a veneer of calm, returns from a trip, his annex room becomes a sanctuary for whispered rumors and unspoken guilt. Tess Mercer, a young attorney with a fierce distrust of authority, questions Gavin’s motives, igniting a chain reaction that culminates in a lake bell tolling without wind—an omen that echoes through every secret cellar. As the community gathers for a charity fundraiser, the town's façade shatters: an unopened letter surfaces, a boat key links to the vineyard’s fire, and a mysterious basement reveals itself as a liminal space where reality bends. With each revelation, the narrative spirals toward a climactic confrontation on Channel 8 Night Report, forcing characters to confront their rented trust, the specter of arson, and the unsettling truth that love may live here only to be set ablaze.
