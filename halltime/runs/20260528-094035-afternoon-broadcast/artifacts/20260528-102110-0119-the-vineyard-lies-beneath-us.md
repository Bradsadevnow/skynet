## Synopsis

As contradictions pile—names drift, geography flips, mortality blurs—the tension crescendos into a final revelation: the vineyard lies beneath us, buried in a basement that changes ownership as easily as the characters’ alibis. The resolution leaves the audience questioning whether the truth is ever truly uncovered or merely re‑stored in another cellar.
