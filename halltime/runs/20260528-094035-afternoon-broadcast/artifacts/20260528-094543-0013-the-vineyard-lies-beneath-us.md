## Synopsis
The Vineyard Lies Beneath Us unfurls in the rain‑slick alleys of Blackthorn County, where every house hides a secret deeper than its cellar. Rachel Bell, an architect haunted by an unresolved implosion, inherits a winter‑unused meditation annex and finds herself entangled in a web of whispers—phone static calling her name, a ceramic owl that no longer feels decorative, and a boat key dripping with fresh mud. Tess Marlowe’s disdain for the move sparks suspicion toward Cole Hale, a family physician whose hand‑to‑hand kindness masks a dark agenda. Sienna Cross, the enigmatic realtor, wields old family records as weapons while secretly knowing why the orchard chapel was shut. As the town rallies around a fundraiser and a casserole dish that seems to defy physics, shadows lengthen: the sheriff’s misplaced suspicion, a missing friend who may be dead or alive, and an altar of contradictions about basement ownership. In a climactic march toward The Candle Finch, truth shatters like glass, leaving Rachel questioning whether she is merely the witness or the instrument of Blackthorn’s hidden crime.
