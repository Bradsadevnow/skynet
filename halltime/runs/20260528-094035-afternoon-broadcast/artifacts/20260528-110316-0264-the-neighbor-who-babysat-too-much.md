## Synopsis
The Neighbor Who Babysat Too Much is a haunting, atmospheric melodrama that drips with the uncanny reality of Blackthorn County. Mara Pruitt, a law‑savvy professional who has buried trauma beneath her career, relocates to Alder Ridge only to discover that every home holds an unseen basement—literal and figurative. As she navigates the tangled web of local relationships—a hospital administrator with a hidden past, a real estate agent whose loyalties are as volatile as his fortunes, and a widow carrying documents that should have been burned—the line between protector and predator blurs. The climax converges on an old family office where hidden truths finally surface, leaving Mara—and the audience—to question whether justice can be served when every house hides its own motive.
